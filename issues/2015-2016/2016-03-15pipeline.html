Student Space Fund (SSF) Applications - Call for Additional Submissions

Applications to the Winter 2016 Student Space Fund (SSF) have been reopened and wil be accepted until Friday March 27th at 5:00 PM. The purpose of the SSF is to maintain and improve engineering undergraduate space through capital expenditures. The fund can be used for anything from buying furniture for a departmental lounge to buying plants for the hallways! Any undergraduate engineering student, department, or group can apply to the fund. The applications can be found here, and for questions and submissions please contact the EUS Funds Director at funds.director@mcgilleus.ca

POWE Speed-Networking

POWE is hosting its 6th annual speed-networking event next Wednesday! It is an event for female engineering students to network with up to 30 female engineers. This event is an excellent way to learn networking skills and ask questions about gender bias's in the workplace, work life balance, and different career paths.


Registration is limited to 60 students and spots are going fast so sign up today! Sign up using the following link: http://tinyurl.com/speed-networking2016


CV's will not be accepted at the event. This is more a mentoring opportunity! More details on our Facebook event!

NOBE Annual Business Case Competition

The National Organization for Business and Engineering (NOBE)'s 2nd annual case competition is happening MARCH 27th !

The NOBE Business Case Competition (NBCC) is a full day event in which teams of 4 engineering students will attempt to crack a business case and present their creative solutions to a panel of judges from both industry and academia. Teams will have 3 hours to prepare a presentation and a powerpoint. Presentations will be 15 minutes and will be followed by a 5 minute Q&A with the judges! No experience necessary! Beginners are welcome!

This year, we are proud to announce that our judges will include representatives from CAPITAL ONE, ACCENTURE and BCG. Thinking of applying for an MBA? The YALE MBA Admissions Director will also be giving a presentation about their MBA program and will be happy to answer any questions you may have!

For more details on how to apply check out the FB event here!
