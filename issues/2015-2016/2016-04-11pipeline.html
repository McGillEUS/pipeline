EUS Budget Director Position Re-opened

We are collecting applications for the 2016-17 Budget Director Position!  We're looking for a organized, self-driven individual who is proficient with Excel.  The individual will be responsible with preparing and budgeting $2.3 million in cash flows to create the EUS' annual budget.  The application form can be found here. If you have further questions, please direct them to vpfinance@mcgilleus.ca 
 

The EUS wants you to be our new Space Director!

Common Room, Clubs Hub, Committee Room, the Mall... the EUS manages a lot of space and many are in dire need of renovation and modernization. If you are interested in managing the EUS' long-term vision for space, then apply for our new Space Director position!

As Space Director, you will be in charge of creating a vision for the EUS spaces, consult with the various parties and you will ge the chance to oversee the implementation of your designs. Specifically, the EUS would like to look at plans for an overhaul of the Common Room, located in the McConnell basement, home of our weekly Blues Pub. A new colour scheme for the wall? A new mural? New furniture or games for the games room? Up to you to decide!

Applications are open until Friday, April 15th at 5pm: please submit the application form attached to the email along with your CV to vpservices@mcgilleus.ca. It is strongly recommended that applicants be present in Montreal over the summer to carry out the EUS Common Room space planning project.

Apply for a POWE Scholarship!

Applications for the POWE Leadership Award and the POWE Dedication Award are now open! Both awards are valued at $500, and the deadline to apply is April 25th at 11:59pm. Please see below for more information about each scholarship:


POWE Leadership Award: awarded to a returning female identified engineering student who has displayed leadership qualities and involvement in extracurricular activities outside or within the McGill Community. Application found here!

POWE Dedication Award: awarded to an undergraduate engineering student who has displayed leadership qualities and dedication to the POWE mandate of promoting opportunities for women in engineering. Application found here!
