Conference on Diversity in Engineering 2015 Delegation

CDE aims to encourage discussion and collaboration between engineering students and professionals around the theme of diversity in engineering. Students attending CDE will have the opportunity to meet and hear from successful, interesting, and talented speakers prepared to share their experience and knowledge on how to embrace diversity in order to better address the complex, interdisciplinary problems of society. CDE 2015 will take place in Waterloo, Ontario on November 13-15th.

If you are interested in being part of the EUS delegation, please fill out the attached application form by October 7, and send it to vpexternal@mcgilleus.ca. Interviews will take place October 8.
 

Forum on Diversity and Inclusivity in Engineering

On Tuesday, October 6th, EUS Equity, the Faculty of Engineering, and the Mary Brown Fund will bring together students, faculty, and industry leaders for a Forum on Diversity and Inclusivity in Engineering! The event will take place in Wong 1020 from 6:00-8:00pm.

We’re excited to host this open discussion of gender, LGBTQIA, racial, and cultural diversity within our faculty and the engineering profession at large, and hope that you will join us. Keynote speaker Brie Code, lead programmer at Ubisoft, will begin the forum, followed by discussion from a panel of leaders in the McGill community: Adeola Odusanya (President of NSBE), Tynan Jarrett (SEDE), and Prof. Susan Gaskin (Dept. of Civil Engineering). Refreshments and food will be provided; admission is free. Please join us at this event and in our efforts to create an open conversation on how to create a truly diverse and inclusive community within our faculty.

Not sure what you want to do after you graduate? Looking for work experience? Interested in entrepreneurship or working at a Canadian start-up?
