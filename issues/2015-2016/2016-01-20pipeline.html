**ELECTIONS ANNOUNCEMENT **
 

Become an EUS Executive! Nomination Period Now Open for Executives and SSMU Reps

The Nomination Period for the 2016-2017 EUS Elections beings today! Nominations are open for the positions of President, Vice Presidents, and SSMU Representatives. 

If you are interested in leading the vision and operations of your student society, please print, complete, and return your nomination form to the CRO mailbox in the EUS office (McConnell Engineering Room 7). The nomination form and other important information can be found at:

http://elections.mcgilleus.ca/

With the exception of Vice President Finance (chosen by selection committee), the nomination period ends on Wednesday February 3rd at 5:00PM. The deadline for Vice President Finance application submissions is Wednesday February 17th at 5:00PM. 

Candidates are strongly encouraged to discuss the responsibilties of these positions with a current executive. For questions, please contact cro@mcgilleus.ca!
