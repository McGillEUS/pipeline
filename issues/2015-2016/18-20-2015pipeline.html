MRT HR & Operations Manager recruitment

McGill Rocket Team is looking for an HR & Operations Manager. If you are interested please send your C.V and cover letter to mcgillrocketteam@gmail.com. You can learn more about our team on our social media platoforms and website: mcgillrocketteam.com.

Duties include but not limited to:

- Maintaining and updating the membership list of the team.
- Implementing and maintaining the by-laws.
- Sending the newsletter with team updates.
- Co-signing all meeting minutes with the Team Captain.
- Booking and arranging meeting rooms, organizing and obtaining refreshments, and drafting agendas.

 
 

Free Online Help Sessions for Phys131 Exam

Prep101, McGill's and Canada's best exam prep company, is offering FREE Cram for a Cause Help Sessions to get you prepped for the PHYS131 Midterm and raise money for worthy causes. Donations are optional – not required – and 100% of donations go to Engineers without Borders.

We recommend you take advantage of these help sessions!

The Help Sessions will be taught by an excellent instructor and include a free customized Study Guide with everything you need to know to excel on your Midterm plus a package of past Midterms with full Solutions.
