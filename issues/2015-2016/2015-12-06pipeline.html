CFES is hiring translators!

The Canadian Federation of Engineering Students is looking for two bilingual translators to translate their official documents. The translators are eligible for a share of the Federation's $500 translation fund as compensation for their work. Prospective candidates must be mostly available from December 30th to January 8th to translate PowerPoint presentations, motions, and reports. Afterwards the translators will be asked to occasionally translate emails, social media posts, and other short texts, until March 31st. The application can be found here.
 

The Plumber's Ledger is looking for a new editor!

The Ledger is looking for a new editor for the Winter semester!

Your tasks include managing everything to do with a student-run publication: balancing budgets, fostering communication within the engineering community, finding content and contributors, as well as working with the layouts and editing of the articles.

The position falls under the EUS VP Comm portfolio. Email ledger@mcgilleus.ca if you are interested in finding out more about the position.
