The Plumber's Ledger Looking for Contributors

The Plumber's Ledger, the EUS's student publication, is looking for writers, editors, artists, and anyone else interested in contributing to our paper. The Ledger is a great way for engineers to improve their communication skills (guaranteed to be 100% more fun than CCOM 206) and get involved in the EUS community. It's a great chance to write about whatever you want with minimal editorial oversight! If interested, contact the editor at editor@plumbersledger.ca or through our facebook page.

Engineering Frosh Sponsorship Coordinator Application - Due this Friday!

Preparations for Frosh 2016 are already underway, and the EUS is looking for a sponsorship coordinator for this year's Engineering Frosh committee! This coordinator will be tasked with working with the EUS's corporate relations team to acquire corporate and local sponsorship for Frosh (starting upon being selected), and will be able to also work on other aspects of Frosh once the committee is formed in its entirety. While it is not necessary to be in Montreal over the summer, it will be taken into consideration. If you have any questions, you can direct them to vpinternal@mcgilleus.ca. The deadline to submit this application is February 19th, 6PM!
 

The Canadian Engineering Competition is Looking for Volunteers!

The 2016 Canadian Engineering Competition will be hosted by the Engineering Undergraduate Society of McGill University, from March 3-5 at the Hotel Bonaventure in Downtown Montreal! Nearly 200 Engineering students from all over Canada will join us and compete in seven categories ranging from Junior and Senior Design to Extemporaneous Debates!


Like any event of this size, this Competition will rely heavily on a group of dedicated and enthusiastic volunteers. If you'd like to help out, for the whole weekend or only for a little while, please fill out this form before February 19th 11:59PM:


Selected volunteers will be contacted by email with further instructions. All volunteers must attend one of two info-sessions that will be held on February 23rd and February 25th, at 6PM in the Engineering Common Room.


Thank you for your interest in volunteering at the Canadian Engineering Competition! If you have any questions, please contact Bryan at bryan.gingras@mail.mcgill.ca
