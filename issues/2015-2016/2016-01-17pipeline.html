Work part-time as a 3D printing technician!

The EUS' newly launched 3D printing service (MD264) is hiring an employee!

Do you have experience operating 3D printers and using CAD software? We are looking for an engaged, initiative-taking student with knowledge of additive manufacturing to help us get the service off the ground! Job will entail processing orders, assessing .STL files, and providing consultations to customers.

Please send in your CV and a short blurb about yourself to aml.mecheng@mcgill.ca. Deadline to apply is Wednesday Jan 20th at 5pm. Only candidates selected for an interview will be contacted.

The position is remunerated, and it is expected you would be working around 5-6 hours per week. 

For more information, please visit the website or email aml.mecheng@mcgill.ca

ESC Presents DUSTED

Come one, come all to the adventure you’ve been waiting for. An event like no other, the first of its kind. ESC is hosting it’s very first D.U.S.T.E.D. on Friday, January 29th. You may be asking yourself, "What is D.U.S.T.E.D.?" D.U.S.T.E.D. stands for Drink an Unsuspecting Small Town Establishment Dry. So with that being said, come to the G-Store, reserve your spot on the bus, and pay 15$ to the lovely person behind the counter. You will be able to enjoy a bus trip full of rowdiness and fun with goons included on the way. This will be a great night of fun that no one should miss!

McGill Conference For Engineering Success

On Saturday, January 30th, EUS Junior Council is hosting the McGill Conference For Engineering Success. Taking place in the New Residence Ballroom from 9:30-5:00, the day will consist of listening to guest speakers, hearing from departmental panels, and participating in professional workshops.
