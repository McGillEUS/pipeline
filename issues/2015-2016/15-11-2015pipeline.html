NOBE Presents: Accounting for Engineers

The National Organization of Business and Engineering (NOBE) would like to present our next workshop: ACCOUNTING FOR ENGINEERS happening NOV 16th! Whether you want to learn what "accounting" means, or are interested in exploring career options, this quick one-hour session is sure to help you out!

Our speaker, Michael Haddad, graduated from McGill in April 2014 with an undergraduate degree in finance, and has worked at Deloitte Corporate Finance for a year. Come hear his insight into the world of accounting/corporate finance and he will also be happy to answer any questions you may have!

MIAE Guest Speaker Series November 17th

Come join us at another one of our Guest Speaker series featuring Prof. Damiano Pasini from McGill University and Rushabh Kothari from Bombardier Aerospace.

The topics being presented will be on Integrated Product Development of aerospace components, composites and hybrid materials optimizations, so bring any questions you may have!

3D Printing Hack Night

Interested in 3D Printing? Ever wanted to hack on cool hardware?

Research Commons - Room A in the Redpath Library building just opened up this month as a new space for students and entrepreneurs to work on cool projects! We have 3D Printers, Oculus Rifts and a lot of cool hardware as well as resources for software.
Join us in a short demo of the new hardware and learn how they can and are being used by makers to hack and innovate. Come get a chance to test them out yourself!
Spots are limited, please register your FREE eventbrite ticket (link above) to secure a spot. Also free coffee and dessert will be served!

Date/ Time: Nov 19th, 6~8pm

Location: Redpath Library, 1st Floor, Room 23 (Research Commons)
