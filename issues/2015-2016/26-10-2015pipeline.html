MIAE Aircraft Development Course

MIAE will be offering an Introduction to Aircraft Development course . Starting on October 29, the course will be delivered by three Bombardier engineers on Thursday Evenings from 6 PM to 8 PM. This short course provides an overview of key phases in the product development cycle of an aircraft. .


The course will cover the following topics:


• Aircraft Conceptual Design (Thursday October 29, 6 PM to 8 PM)
• Aircraft Integration with an emphasis on electrical systems (Thursday November 5, 6 PM to 8 PM)
• Risk Management (Thursday November 19, 6 PM to 8 PM)

To subscribe please register here, and note that there is a $20 deposit which will then be returned if you attend at least two lectures.

You can pay your deposit in room FDA6 on Tuesday Oct. 27 from 14:00 to 16:00 and Wednesday Oct. 28 from 15:00 to 17:00.

The CFES is looking to recruit an Official Languages Commissioner!

The Canadian Federation of Engineering Students is a national student organization that represents approximately 60,000 engineering students across Canada. The CFES is looking for a motivated Engineering student to join the Federation's officer team as Official Languages Commissioner.

The Official Language Commissioner is responsible for ensuring that CFES operations. This includes coordinating all text translation services for the CFES officer team, promoting bilingualism within CFES and raising awareness of both official languages and relevant language issues. The chosen candidate will also work with the National Executive to form an Official Languages Committee that will be responsible for handling a majority of the CFES's translation duties.

If you are interested in this position, please fill out this application form before 11:59PM on Monday, November 2nd, Eastern Time. For more information, or if you have any questions, please get in touch with Bryan at bryan.gingras@cfes.ca.

The application form can be found here.
