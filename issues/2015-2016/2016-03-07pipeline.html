Visit to Montreal’s Jean-R. Marcotte Wastewater Treatment Plant by POWE!!!

Are you interested in wastewater treatment? Have you ever wondered what amazing job opportunities are offered at such a facility?

POWE is organizing an industry tour to Montreal’s Jean-R. Marcotte Wastewater Treatment Plant on Wednesday, March 16th and would love for you to join!

We will be leaving by bus from Milton Gates at around 12:00 PM and returning by 4:45 PM.

Sign up will be available as of Monday, March 7th at the G-Store and a refundable fee of $5 will be asked upon registration. There are only 25 spots available, so ACT FAST!

All engineering students are welcome!
 

Septodont is Recruiting!

Septodont is a pharmaceutical company which develops and manufactures a wide range of high quality products for dental professionals.

We are looking for highly motivated engineering students who want to gain significant experience in project management and manufacturing.

Attend the info session on March 14th @ 12:30pm in FDA 5 to discover more about the internship opportunities at Septodont.

If you think you have what it takes to be part of our next intern team, send your resume to ctrudeau@septodont.com. Don't forget to also bring it to the info-session!
