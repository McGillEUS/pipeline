VP Finance, Exec and SSMU Rep Nominations Open Until Feb 3rd

The Nomination Period for the 2016-2017 EUS Elections is open until February 3rd! Nominations are open for the positions of President, Vice Presidents, and SSMU Representatives.

With the exception of VP Finance (chosen by selection committee), the nomination period ends on Wednesday February 3rd at 5:00PM. The deadline for VP Finance application submissions is Wednesday February 17th at 5:00PM. Please submit VP Finance applications electronically to president@mcgilleus.ca.

For all other positions: please print, complete, and return your nomination form to the CRO mailbox in the EUS office (McConnell Engineering Room 7). All forms and details can be found here.

Candidates are strongly encouraged to discuss the responsibilities of these positions with a current executive. For any questions, please contact cro@mcgilleus.ca.

The Canadian Engineering Competition is Looking for Volunteers!

The 2016 Canadian Engineering Competition will be hosted by the Engineering Undergraduate Society of McGill University, from March 3-5 at the Hotel Bonaventure in Downtown Montreal! Nearly 200 Engineering students from all over Canada will join us and compete in seven categories ranging from Junior and Senior Design to Extemporaneous Debates!


Like any event of this size, this Competition will rely heavily on a group of dedicated and enthusiastic volunteers. If you'd like to help out, for the whole weekend or only for a little while, please fill out this form before February 19th 11:59PM.


Selected volunteers will be contacted by email with further instructions. All volunteers must attend one of two info-sessions that will be held on February 23rd and February 25th, at 6PM in the Engineering Common Room.


Thank you for your interest in volunteering at the Canadian Engineering Competition! If you have any questions, please contact Bryan at bryan.gingras@mail.mcgill.ca

ESC Presents DUSTED

Come one, come all to the adventure you’ve been waiting for. An event like no other, the first of its kind. ESC is hosting it’s very first D.U.S.T.E.D. on Friday, January 29th. You may be asking yourself, "What is D.U.S.T.E.D.?" D.U.S.T.E.D. stands for Drink an Unsuspecting Small Town Establishment Dry. So with that being said, come to the G-Store, reserve your spot on the bus, and pay 15$ to the lovely person behind the counter. You will be able to enjoy a bus trip full of rowdiness and fun with special beverages included on the way. This will be a great night of fun that no one should miss!
