Mental Health Commissioner

EUS is looking for a Mental Health Commissioner whose mandate will be to raise awareness about Mental Health among the engineering student body and to create resources tailored to engineering student needs. The Commissioner will also be working with other McGill groups that tackles Mental Health and advertise relevant activities to the EUS.

Applications are due March 20th at 5:00pm, and forms can be found here!
 

External Relations Director Application

Want to make a difference and meet fellow engineering students from across Canada?

Apply for the External Relations Director position and get the unique opportunity to meet student leaders from across Quebec at the Confédération pour le rayonnement étudiant en ingénierie au Quebec (CRÉIQ) and across Canada at the Canadian Federation of Engineering Students (CFES). You will also be working on provincial and national projects which have a direct impact on engineering undergraduates

Application ends on March 20th at 5:00pm, and forms can be found here!
 

McGill Engineering Competition Chair Application

The McGill Engineering Competition is an exciting interdisciplinary engineering competition that allows students of all types of engineering to test their skills in varying competitions. As Chair, your role will be to lead the committee and plan a successful edition of MEC in November 2016. Last year’s edition hit a record 140 participants so the bar is high!

Application ends on March 20th at 5:00pm, and forms can be found here!
 

Apply to be TechWeek Chair 2017!

Do you want to run the next TechWeek? You can apply now for the position of TechWeek Chair for 2017!

TechWeek is a week-long of events hosted in January by the EUS, aimed to explore where an engineering degree can take you. From TED-like professor TechTalks, to debates, workshops, and cutting-edge technology demos, TechWeek is the place to showcase all opportunities available in engineering at McGill and in Montreal.

Any returning student is encouraged to apply! For more information about the position, please get in touch with this year's TechWeek Chair, Claire Reierson, at techweek@mcgilleus.ca. You can also come hear more about the position at Tuesday's "Get Involved in EUS" info-session.

To apply, please fill out this application form and email it to the EUS VP Academic at vpacademic@mcgilleus.ca. Deadline to apply is this Friday, March 18th at 5pm.
