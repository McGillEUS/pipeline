VP Finance, Exec and SSMU Rep Nominations Open Until Feb 3rd

The Nomination Period for the 2016-2017 EUS Elections is open until February 3rd! Nominations are open for the positions of President, Vice Presidents, and SSMU Representatives.

With the exception of VP Finance (chosen by selection committee), the nomination period ends on Wednesday February 3rd at 5:00PM. The deadline for VP Finance application submissions is Wednesday February 17th at 5:00PM. Please submit VP Finance applications electronically to president@mcgilleus.ca.

For all other positions: please print, complete, and return your nomination form to the CRO mailbox in the EUS office (McConnell Engineering Room 7). All forms and details can be found at:

http://elections.mcgilleus.ca/

Candidates are strongly encouraged to discuss the responsibilities of these positions with a current executive. For any questions, please contact cro@mcgilleus.ca.

POWErful Alumni Speaker Series

POWE is excited to announce our fourth POWErful Alumni Speaker series. Join us on February 4th from 6:00 - 8:00 PM in TR0070 for a inspiring talk on "Unlocking Your Potential". Sign up using the Google form attached!

Our speaker is Léa Poquérusse. Léa obtained her B. Eng, Chemical Engineering from McGill and then completed a M.Eng in Chemical Engineering from MIT. She is currently pursuing an MBA at the Stanford Graduate Business School and has worked on developing energy projects for a consulting firm, from Toronto to Dubai.

Lea will tell us her story of just how circuitous 6 years out of McGill can be. She will also share insights on how to best prepare and manage the unknown road ahead, how to set and achieve your goals and the importance of mentoring in her life.

Bring all your questions about life after graduation and come meet an inspiring alumna! Register to attend here!
