EQUIPMENT FUND RENEWAL REFERENDUM

The Engineering Undergraduate Equipment Fund is a fund from 1987 that provides undergraduate students with additional lab equipment, computers, 3D printers, and tons of other cool stuff. View the full list of equipment available from the past few years here.

The existence of the Equipment Fund must be put to referendum once every four years, and that time has come. The EUS Council would like to renew the Equipment Fund by renewing the non opt-outable fee of $50/student per semester for full-time students (9 credits or more), and $25/student per semester for part-time students (less than 9 credits).

Please note that a majority NO vote will lead to the termination of the fee and all investments by the EUS into equipment for undergraduate students. Your active involvement in this referendum is incredibly important.

Please feel free to stop by the EUS Office (McConnell 7) if you have any questions, email any member of the EUS exec team, or check out the informational table in McConnell all of this week!

We will be hosting a town hall on Wednesday, November 11 at 5:30 pm in FDA 6B, so please join us if you have any questions or are interested in learning more about the Equipment Fund!

Voting will take place from Saturday, November 14 at 12:00 pm until Wednesday, November 18 at 5:00 pm. Keep an eye out for your ballot in your email inbox!

EWB Junior Fellowship in International Development

Every year, EWB McGill sends one student overseas as part of the organization's Junior Fellowship in International Development. The Fellowship is an 18-month-long commitment, with six months of predeparture learning, four months working in either Africa or Canada and eight months of sharing one's experience and using it to create change in their own community once returning home.

The program is a fantastic opportunity for anyone looking to challenge themselves, develop their skills and dive deep into the realities of development on the ground! All costs are entirely covered by EWB and applications are currently being accepted by students in ALL FACULTIES.

If you would like to apply, simply fill out this application.

Deadline: Sunday November 15th.

Please email Jube at julia-annreyes@ewb.ca if you have any questions about the program or application process.
