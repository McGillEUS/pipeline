Fall 2015 Referendum Results

The polls are closed and the results are in!

The Question on the renewal of the Equipment Fund passed with 82.17% of the vote in favour of renewal, and with a voter turnout of 27.79%. This means that the Equipment Fund will remain in existance up to and including the Fall 2020 semester.

Sunnybrook Research Prize: call for applicants

Each year, SRI hosts the Sunnybrook Research Prize competition for
third- and fourth-year undergraduate students in the physical sciences and engineering disciplines. The competition recognizes excellence in undergraduate research with a specific focus on biomedical research. Ten finalists will be selected to present their research projects to SRI scientists, staff and trainees. The winner will receive a $10,000 cash prize.

The deadline for this year's Sunnybrook Research Prize competition has been extended to December 4, 2015. Details about the the competition and how to apply can be found online here.
