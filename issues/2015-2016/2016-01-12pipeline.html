MIAE Guest Speaker: CAE Senior Executive

MIAE Guest Speaker: CAE Senior Executive

On January 18th from 6pm – 7pm, in McConnell 13, join us to our first guest speaker series of the semester with Sylvain Beaudry, a senior executive at CAE. This will be a great opportunity to hear about the career path of an executive in the aerospace industry.

Mr. Beaudry is the Global PMO Director at CAE and a Senior Project Manager with 14 years of experience in management, portfolio management, project management, process development, and strategic planning. Prior to joining CAE he held several managerial positions at Bombardier. He obtained a bachelor in Mechanical Engineering from École Polytechnique de Montréal and a masters in project management from Université du Québec à Montréal.

Apply for SSMU Funding!

Student groups at McGill can apply for funding from one of SSMU’s eight funds for events or projects. The funding process has been modified this term; each application must now be for ONE event and for ONE fund. There will also be a tiered deadline this year. The deadline for Ambassador, Charity, First Year, Green, and Space Fund applications is January 22nd, 2016 at 11:59pm. The deadline for Campus Life, Club and Equity Fund applications is February 11th, 2016 at 11:59pm. For this term’s application form and more information check out our website.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact the Funding Commissioner at fundcom@ssmu.mcgill.ca
