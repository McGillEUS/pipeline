01. McGill Conference For Engineering Success
 

McGill Conference For Engineering Success

** Apologies, this blurb got cut off in the previously sent out Pipeline. In the interests of fairly representing this event, the full blurb can be found below. Thanks! **

On Saturday, January 30th, EUS Junior Council is hosting the McGill Conference For Engineering Success. The conference aims to help students develop skills and make connections necessary for success in their field. Taking place in the New Residence Ballroom from 9:30-5:00, the day will consist of listening to guest speakers, hearing from departmental panels, and participating in professional workshops. Keynote speaker Sam Haffar is a representative from Real Ventures, and other speakers have experience working for Facebook, Apple, Microsoft and more! For more information, click here!
