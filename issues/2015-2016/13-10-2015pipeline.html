Apply here.

MRT HR & Operations Manager recruitment

McGill Rocket Team is looking for an HR & Operations Manager. If you are interested please send your C.V and cover letter to mcgillrocketteam@gmail.com. You can learn more about our team on our social media platoforms and website: mcgillrocketteam.com.

Duties include but not limited to:

- Maintaining and updating the membership list of the team.
- Implementing and maintaining the by-laws.
- Sending the newsletter with team updates.
- Co-signing all meeting minutes with the Team Captain.
- Booking and arranging meeting rooms, organizing and obtaining refreshments, and drafting agendas.

From Imagination to Reality

Racine Creations 3D is offering an immersive workshop on 3D printing. From imagination to reality will be held in Outremont, Montreal, the weekend of November 14 and 15. Become a 3D printing expert through the weekend long hands-on workshop. Build and leave with your own industrial grade 3D printer. Learn best practices for designing for additive manufacturing and master printer settings for assured perfect prints of all your creations. Registrations ends Wednesday, October 14, 2015. Places are limitted. Although the course description is in French, English speakers are welcome, the instructor is fluent in both French and English. You will find all the details of the course on the registration page.


For more information, please contact Ethienne Racine at

info@racinecreations3d.com
