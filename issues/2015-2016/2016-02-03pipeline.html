EUS Elections: Nomination Period Extended

The nomination period has been extended to 5:00PM this Friday, February 5th, for the positions of VP Academic, VP Clubs and Administration, VP Communications, VP External, and SSMU Representative.

Please print, complete, and return your nomination form to the CRO mailbox in the EUS office (McConnell Engineering Room 7). All forms and details can be found online here.

Candidates are strongly encouraged to discuss the responsibilities of these positions with a current executive. For any questions, please contact cro@mcgilleus.ca.
