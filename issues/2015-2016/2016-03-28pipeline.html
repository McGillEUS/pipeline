POWE Visit to SAP

QE VP Election

Are you an engineering student who also happens to be queer (or a very motivated ally/non-engineering queer)? Do you want to take on some responsibility in a great club running under EUS? Well lucky you, because Queer Engineer will be holding elections for its executive positions for the fall 2016/winter 2017 semesters! The possible positions are President, VP Internal, VP Communications, VP Admin and VP Finance. If you wish to apply or have any questions, email us at queer.engineer@mcgilleus.ca at least 3 days before the election day, which will be on April 11th at 6pm in the Committee Room, in McConnell. For more information on the positions, please refer to our Facebook event QE VP Elections. We hope to hear from you!
