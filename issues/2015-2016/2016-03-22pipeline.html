TechWeek Chair Application: Deadline extended

Do you want to run the next TechWeek? You can apply now for the position of TechWeek Chair for 2017!

TechWeek is a week-long of events hosted in January by the EUS, aimed to explore where an engineering degree can take you. From TED-like professor TechTalks, to debates, workshops, and cutting-edge technology demos, TechWeek is the place to showcase all opportunities available in engineering at McGill and in Montreal.

Any returning student is encouraged to apply! For more information about the position, please get in touch with this year's TechWeek Chair, Claire Reierson, at techweek@mcgilleus.ca.

To apply, please fill out this application form and email it to the EUS VP Academic at vpacademic@mcgilleus.ca. Deadline to apply is this Wednesday, March 23rd at 5pm.

POWE Speed-Networking

POWE is hosting its 6th annual speed-networking event tomorrow! It is an event for female engineering students to network with up to 30 female engineers. This event is an excellent way to learn networking skills and ask questions about gender bias's in the workplace, work life balance, and different career paths.


Registration is limited to 60 students and spots are going fast so sign up today! Sign up using the following link: http://tinyurl.com/speed-networking2016


CV's will not be accepted at the event. This is more a mentoring opportunity!

More details on our Facebook event!
