PPO Presents Golden Valentines

It’s time to have your loved ones serenaded by the musically semi-talented Plumbers’ Philharmonic Orchestra with all proceeds going to The Heart & Stroke Foundation! On Friday, February 12th, the PPO will be going to classes providing serenades, cupcakes, flowers, love notes, balloons, and much more to your friends and professors! This year, we’re getting help from various acapella groups, bands, and baking groups on campus. We will be tabling in the McConnell Hallway from Monday, February 8th until Thursday, February 11th. Be sure to check out our Facebook page for more information!
 

Town Hall: What Do You Think of a Smoke-Free Campus?

Students are gathering feedback on preventative smoking policies at McGill! Current proposals include cessation programs, education campaigns, outdoor smoking shelters, and a smoke-free campus. Come learn more and share your thoughts at a town hall consultation on Tuesday, February 9th at 5:30pm in the Madeleine Parent Room of SSMU. This event is open to all members of the McGill community. Light refreshments will be served. Check out the Facebook event!

Volunteer at POWE Conference 2016!

POWE Conference 2016 is now looking for volunteers!

On Feb 12 2016, POWE will be hosting a conference for high school girls interested in pursuing a degree in engineering. To showcase how awesome our engineering program is, POWE has planned an eventful day including speaker series, a design fair, lab tours, a mini design competition and our newest event- lunch networking, where you can talk to the girls about your McGill engineering experience!
As the conference is a daylong event, we will NEED volunteers for various aspects of the day. To sign up for a shift (or multiple-there's no limit) please fill out this form!
 

The Canadian Engineering Competition is Looking for Volunteers!

The 2016 Canadian Engineering Competition will be hosted by the Engineering Undergraduate Society of McGill University, from March 3-5 at the Hotel Bonaventure in Downtown Montreal! Nearly 200 Engineering students from all over Canada will join us and compete in seven categories ranging from Junior and Senior Design to Extemporaneous Debates!


Like any event of this size, this Competition will rely heavily on a group of dedicated and enthusiastic volunteers. If you'd like to help out, for the whole weekend or only for a little while, please fill out this form before February 19th 11:59PM!


Selected volunteers will be contacted by email with further instructions. All volunteers must attend one of two info-sessions that will be held on February 23rd and February 25th, at 6PM in the Engineering Common Room.


Thank you for your interest in volunteering at the Canadian Engineering Competition! If you have any questions, please contact Bryan at bryan.gingras@mail.mcgill.ca

GE Industry Tour by POWE

Are you interested in robotics, advanced manufacturing and/or aeronautics? Have you ever wondered what amazing job opportunities are offered at GE?

POWE is organizing an industry tour to GE’s Bromont Aviation Facility on Thursday, February 18th and would love for you to join!

We will be leaving by bus from McGill at around 7:30 AM and returning by 12:30 PM.

Sign up will be available as of Tuesday, February 2nd at the G-Store and a refundable fee of $5 will be asked upon registration. This is a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity, so ACT FAST! Last day to sign up is Monday, February 8th!

All engineering students are welcome!
 

NSBE Grad. School Panel

Do you want to know if grad school is for you? Are you considering attending grad school but aren't sure where to begin? Are you an international student? If any of these apply to you, then this Panel is for you.

NSBE, in collaboration with MESC, is hosting a Graduate School Panel on 18th February at 5.30pm. We will have panelists that have or are completing graduate school answer your numerous questions and concerns about graduate school. Samosas will be served. The venue is FDA 5.
