SuitsU Montreal Spring Suit Sale

March 28
Service
Event

Got an interview, job or internship coming up but don't have the perfect suit? Suits U Montreal is here to help with our annual Dress To Impress Suit Sale!

We offer gently worn, high quality, good condition business wear for men and women sold at a fraction of their original price! All proceeds go to charity. When? Thursday, March 28th 2019 from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Where? Location will be announced soon. We will be accepting cash and Interac e-transfers. Don't miss out on this amazing opportunity!

Suits U is a not-for-profit student initiative dedicated to delivering confidence to students by providing them with high quality, brand-name professional apparel at prices sensitive to their budgets. We source the apparel through suit drives held at professional firms in Montreal and donate our proceeds to local charities. Last year we donated over $2000 to Sun Youth!

Check out our Facebook event.

SHAD McGill 2019, looking for Program Assistants

April 10
Occupational
Application

SHAD McGill is a summer program where 64 top-notch high school students come to McGill for a STEAM-based program for the month of July. We invite leading figures in the academic world as guest speakers, we visit various labs around McGill, and well as sample much that Montreal has to offer. Further, we challenge these students with a "wicked problem" and walk them through design thinking. If you are interested in working as part of the team putting this program on, please contact Bruce Tracy, Program Director bruce.tracy@johnabbott.qc.ca. [Fun fact: Bruce was the founder and first editor of the Pipeline.]
