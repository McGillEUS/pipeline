Fun Fact

This issue's Fun Fact comes from the EUS' history. You can find many tidbits like this over at the EUSwiki.

OAP began in the 1980s, but it became a purely EUS event in 2009. Previously, OAP had been a joint event, where the SSMU procured sponsorship and the EUS handled all other aspects of the event.

Locker Rentals

September 4
Rental
Service

Need a place to store your stuff on campus? Starting today, September 4th, lockers will be available for rent at the EUS Office! Choose from two sizes at four locations:

FDA - Full Size $45
McConnell - Half & Full Size, $35 & $45
Trottier - Half Size $35
Wong - Half Size $35

All prices include a $20 deposit which will be returned at the end of the year upon cleaning out the locker and returning the lock provided.

The EUS Office is located in McConnell Room 7 and will be selling rentals from 8:30 am - 12:00 pm & 1:00 pm - 4:30 pm.

For more information check out Locker Rentals on the EUS Wiki.
