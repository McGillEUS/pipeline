Order form for the next pick-up

Join the Office of the Principal in Bicentennial Preparations!
September 27
Involvement
Info Session

In 2021, McGill University will commemorate its 200th anniversary. The Bicentennial is a chance for all of us at McGill and in Montréal to come together and celebrate the university's history, our rich and diverse community, and look forward to our third century. The Office of the Principal sincerely invites all students to join us in the preparations leading up to the celebrations. We will be holding town halls for the student body to share their ideas, visions and initiate a long-term conversation about the Bicentennial and our shared future. Two town halls will be held downtown, in Leacock 232 from 4pm to 5:30pm: on Wednesday, September 26th and Tuesday, October 16th. Snacks, coffee and refreshments will be provided (Bring-Your-Own-Mug!) on a first-come, first-serve basis. If you would like to correspond in the meanwhile, please feel free to share your suggestions, ideas and concerns at bicentennial.students@mcgill.ca.

 

Cansbridge Fellowship Info Session
September 26
Entrepreneurship
Event

Cansbridge Fellowship applications are now open for the 2019 program!

If you or anyone you know aims to be a global leader, entrepreneur or innovator - head over to https://www.cansbridgefellowship.com/apply to apply.

As a selected Fellow, you receive:

  • A $6,000 scholarship to travel and gain valuable work experience in Asia.

  • Free flights and hotel stay during our annual San Francisco conference where you'll engage with entrepreneurs, industry professionals, startups and investors.

  • Access to a dynamic, encouraging alumni network living across the world.

  • Access to support, capital and connections that will help you develop global companies, technologies and teams.



This program offers the rare skills, experiences and network needed to build and innovate on a global scale. It's a no-brainer for anyone who sees themselves changing the world but simply needs the toolkit to make it happen.

For more information, come out to the info session and pitch competition happening on Sept 26.

McGill Masters of Management in Analytics Info Session
September 24
Academic
Notice

Registration is required to attend. Registration link

INFORMATION:
Join Professor Mehmet Gumus, Academic Director, for a one-hour information session outlining the new Masters of Management in Analytics program at McGill University's Desautels Faculty of Management.

Date: Wednesday, September 26, 2018
Time: 6:00 PM EST
Location: Room 060, Armstrong Building

About the Masters of Management in Analytics:
The McGill Masters of Management in Analytics (MMA) degree is a pre-experience, twelve-month specialized program in the evolving field of management analytics. The MMA program is designed to address the growing needs of organizations (for-profit, non-profit, governmental) for business analytics. The emphasis of the program will be on the managerial problem–solving using state-of-the-art data analytics tools to optimize organizational decisions in a variety of managerial settings.
