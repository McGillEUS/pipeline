Cansbridge Fellowship

January 31
Entrepreneurship
Application

McGill Engineers - applications to the Cansbridge Fellowship have re-opened until January 31st. What is Cansbridge? Every year, the Cansbridge Fellowship accepts 15 highly ambitious Canadian students into the Fellowship. Our programs are designed to accelerate Fellows’ careers, expand their networks and help them develop a global mindset. Fellows experience a summer internship in Asia, a week-long boot camp and conference in San Francisco, and gain a valuable network that lasts a lifetime. Apply now!
