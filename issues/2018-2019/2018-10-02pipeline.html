Academic
Service

The Engineering Peer Tutoring Service is a free tutoring service for U0 and U1 engineering students sponsored by the Faculty of Engineering. Tutorial office hours are held on a weekly basis in the McGill Engineering Student Centre on the ground floor of the Frank Dawson Adams Building, Room 6B.

Register for SKILLS21!
Involvement
Registration

Register now for SKILLS21, the workshop-based skills development program for McGill's undergrads! With SKILLS21 you can choose from a variety of free workshops to develop transferable skills, like teamwork, financial literacy, and time management. You can also focus your learning into one of the program's five skill-area streams: Citizenship, Collaboration, Discovery, Leadership, and Wellbeing. Plus, all of your participation in SKILLS21 is recognized on your co-curricular record! Visit the website to learn more about the program and register today.
