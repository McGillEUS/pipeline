Lockers Available in McConnell

February 4
Service
Rental

Need a place to store your stuff on campus? New lockers have been installed in McConnell and are now available to rent. To rent a locker please come to the EUS Office (McConnell Room 7). We're open Mon-Fri 8:30-12:00 & 1:00-4:30

The cost is a $20 deposit (returned at the end of the semester) plus a $10 rental fee for a total payment of $30. Payment is in cash only.

For more information check out Locker Rentals on the EUS Wiki.

2019 #SelfCareCelebration

February 4

The #SelfCareCelebration (formerly known as the #SelfCareChallenge) is Healthy McGill's 6th annual event aimed at encouraging students to practice new ways of taking care of themselves and their peers. As with everything at Healthy McGill, we advocate for you finding and doing what's best for you! We're here to help support you in whatever that is!

The 2019 Self Care Celebration will run for 12 days, February 4 - 15, with five self care activities to choose from every day. Do one, all five, or make up your own, and tell us about it on Facebook, Instagram, or Snapchat using #SelfCareCelebration.
