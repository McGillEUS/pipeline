Base11 Business Team Recruitment

November 7
Involvement
Application

The McGill Rocket Team is undertaking a new project called "Base11". The goal is to build the first student-built rocket to cross the Karman Line and compete for $1M prize money. To make this possible, the business portfolio is looking for a team of motivated students who would be willing to help out with sponsorship, financial management and public outreach for the project. The goal is to develop a small, motivated interest group who can work in a flat hierarchy and build the Base11 project's business vision.

If you're an engineering student (or any other program) and want to amalgamate your business interest, then this opportunity is for you. Fill out this short interest form, after which you will be contacted for an interview.

Hult Prize at McGill

December 1
Entrepreneurship
Competition

The Hult Prize is a social entrepreneurship competition that happens yearly at McGill. It was invented to inspire people of the university level to change the world through business. Yearly, teams of 3 to 4 people compete with the opportunity to go to regionals and then possibly abroad. This year the competition is happening December 1, 2018 with location TBD. The challenge is to create jobs for 10,000 jobs for youth. To find team members please join the Facebook group: Hult Prize at McGill - Matchmaking and like the Facebook page Hult Prize at McGill - Competition for more updates! Deadline to sign up a team is November 20th!

NOBE Business Case Competition

November 18
Involvement
Competition

The NOBE Business Case Competition is back for another year! Partnering with the McGill Consulting Association (MCA), NBCC is a great opportunity to get together with 3 friends and compete in a case cracking competition. You will develop valuable, real world networking and case cracking skills, while receiving feedback from industry professionals on your work.

Teams of management and engineering students will have three hours to prepare a creative solution to a business case and present their case to judges. In addition to gaining case cracking experience, you'll have the opportunity to network and make new connections with industry professionals. We'd like to thank our sponsors BCG, Capital One, Bain and Deloitte.

You'll also be competing for a $400 CASH PRIZE for the winning team! (100$/person)

No experience is necessary for this event and first timers are encouraged to come out and get a taste of what case competitions are all about. There will be a workshop on how to crack cases before the competition kicks off.

DETAILS
- There will be 20 teams, 4 of which will be composed entirely of management students and the other 16 teams can have max 1 management student.
- Cost is $10/person and includes breakfast, lunch, coffee, and swag.
- You can register as a team (4 per team) or as an individual and we will find you a team.
- Spots are limited! Your team's spot isn't reserved until everyone has paid.

HOW TO REGISTER & PAY
1. Register here.
2. Pay EITHER through the Yapsody (TBA) OR by cash in the EUS Mall from Monday- Friday 10AM-1PM for the next two weeks!
