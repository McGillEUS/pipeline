Student Sponsorship Program

August 1
Involvement
Application

Hey McGillians, do you know that you and your student group can receive funding for your project? Ranging from $250 to $750, the MAA Student Sponsorship Program provides our students with financial support for projects that promote outside-the-classroom student experiences or student-alumni engagement opportunities! To apply, please fill out the application located here and send it to studentprograms.dev@mcgill.ca before August 1st to receive sponsorship for your event or initiative!

August 27
Involvement
Application

Are you looking forward to helping all the fresh incoming students get oriented to McGill and Montreal? Well look no further! We are looking for Facilitators (to lead groups of 25 students for a day) and Logistical Volunteers (to set-up, take-down and make sure the event runs smoothly) for the first event of O-Week. Off-Campus Connects (OCX) is an O-Week event that is offered to incoming students who will NOT be living in a McGill Residence. If you're interested and available on August 27th, 2018 apply here!

EAC is a committee dedicated to organizing diverse events that showcase the variety of fun things Montreal has to offer, with a specific focus on non alcohol-centric events. Past events have included a Food Crawl, games night at Randolph's, trips to La Ronde, camping, rafting, visits to Escape Rooms, rock climbing, snow tubing, laser tag and more!!

The EUS is looking for the next EAC chair to lead the committee in planning and executing more fun, creative events for the 2018-2019 school year! Apply with the link below!
