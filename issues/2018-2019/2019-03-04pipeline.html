March 18
Involvement
Event

Want to get involved? Not sure in what, or how to go about it? Well, this event is for you!

Come out to Involvement Night on Monday, March 18th from 6-8PM to meet lots of people involved in all kinds of EUS groups, including departmental societies, committees, clubs, and design teams. Hey EUS! Voting period for the EUS General Elections have started. You can follow the instructions in your McGill mail or go to ssmu.simplyvoting.com. MSERT Conference - NCCER

March 8
Involvement
Event

The National Conference for Campus Emergency Responders (NCCER) is an annual conference held under the Association of Campus Emergency Response Teams (ACERT). This year, MSERT will be hosting the conference! Throughout the conference, various workshops and lectures are held which address a topics in first aid and student health, such as mental health and LGBTQ+ care. A large portion of the conference is dedicated to encouraging the different campus teams to showcase their skills in a friendly competition, where they respond to a variety of simulations designed to challenge them.

Volunteers are greatly needed to help this entire process run smoothly and would be able to attend the workshops and lectures of their choice. Various roles include actors for scenarios, wayfinders, and general volunteers! To sign-up to volunteer, please follow this link.

If there are any further questions, please do not hesitate to contact the volunteer coordinator, Sabrina, at volunteer@nccer2019.com.
