Lockers Available in McConnell

February 4
Service
Rental

Need a place to store your stuff on campus? New lockers have been installed in McConnell and are now available to rent. To rent a locker please come to the EUS Office (McConnell Room 7). We're open Mon-Fri 8:30-12:00 & 1:00-4:30

The cost is a $20 deposit (returned at the end of the semester) plus a $10 rental fee for a total payment of $30. Payment is in cash only.

For more information check out Locker Rentals on the EUS Wiki.

 

The McGill Space Group is recruiting Software & Mechanical Engineering students!

Involvement
Application

The McGill Space Group CSDC team is currently in the process of designing and building a 3U CubeSat to compete in the Canadian Satellite Design Challenge, a competition for Canadian Universities to build nano-satellites under the CubeSat specification.  We are currently looking for highly-motivated student to join us, specifically, software & mechanical engineering students.

You should apply if you:
- Are interested in aerospace development, specifically satellite design.
- Want to get hands-on experience with spacecraft development.
- Are self-motivated and able to work both in an independent manner and as part of a team.
- Have experience in C programming and/or low-level software development
- Have experience with Finite Element Models and/or Thermal Analysis

If you are interested, please fill in the following form.
