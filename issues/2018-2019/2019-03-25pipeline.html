Dianne Ferguson Involvement Award Application

March 18
Involvement
Application

Hi folks!

The annual Dianne Ferguson Involvement Award is now accepting applications. If you are an EUS member, involved in the McGill community, and has a passion about EUS involvement, this award is for you!

In order to apply, fill out this application form and Include:

- Description of your current and past extra-curricular activities and hobbies within the McGill community.
- Personal statement (max 500 words) answering the question: “What does involvement in the McGill community mean to you?”
- A letter of recommendation from at least one person in the McGill community.
- Any additional information you feel that the committee should consider (max 1 page). The deadline for application is 11:59 pm on March 22nd, 2019. Please email this application form along with the other required documents to vpacademic@mcgilleus.ca (or place them in the VP Academic mailbox in the EUS office).

NOBE x MDN: BAE Case Competition

March 23
Occupational
Competition

Come out on March 23rd to compete in the Business Analytics in Engineering Case Competition, hosted by NOBE and the McGill Data Network! The competition is based on the analysis of a dataset and leads to the development of a business solution. This is a great opportunity for you to practice your skills with data analytics, or gain an introduction to it while tackling a real business problem from EY!

The event will kick off with a workshop on Microsoft’s PowerBI software, followed by the case release on the evening of March 21st. Participants will then have until March 23rd to prepare their presentation. The event is presented by Microsoft and the case is provided by EY. Other corporate sponsors include PwC and BMO Capital Markets.

The registration and payment deadline is March 18th!
