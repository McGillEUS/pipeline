Do you have experience with mental health advocacy? Want to work to share mental health resources and reduce stigma in the engineering community? Consider applying for Mental Health Director! External Relations Director

Job Board
March 30
Involvement
Application

The EUS VP External is looking for a director! The External Relations Director is a position dedicated to assisting the EUS VP External in liaising with other engineering schools on both the provincial (CREIQ/QCESO) and national (CFES) scale. The External Relations Director must be fluent in both English and French, as a majority of the correspondences from CREIQ are in French

If this sounds interesting, apply here! For any questions, please contact external.relations@mcgilleus.ca.

EUS Handbook Editor

Job Board
March 22
Involvement
Application

The EUS Handbook showcases what the EUS is, lists campus resources, and provides useful tools for every academic year. It's a project that is around 100 pages, including an agenda, EUS Group Descriptions, and more. The handbook has infinite possibilities, and it's at the Editor's discretion to guide the direction. The handbook is due in July 2019, and the Handbook editor receives a stipend of $250 for their work. View previous handbooks here!

View this job on the EUS Job Board.
