Lockers Available in McConnell

February 4
Service
Rental

Need a place to store your stuff on campus? New lockers have been installed in McConnell and are now available to rent. To rent a locker please come to the EUS Office (McConnell Room 7). We're open Mon-Fri 8:30-12:00 & 1:00-4:30

The cost is a $20 deposit (returned at the end of the semester) plus a $10 rental fee for a total payment of $30. Payment is in cash only.

For more information check out Locker Rentals on the EUS Wiki.

 

MAME Wine and Cheese

February 22
Networking
Event

MAME is thrilled to present our biggest professional event of the year: MAME Wine and Cheese

Come join us on Friday February 22nd at the Thomson house from 6-8 and network with PROFESSIONALS, ALUMNI, and PROFESSORS over cocktails and cheese. Discover the numerous career paths available to a Mechanical Engineer with leaders in their respective fields.

Representatives from the following companies are expected: EY, MCKINSEY, ASHRAE & TRANE. PROFESSORS HIGGINS and NAHON will also be attending. Stay tuned as other companies and professors confirm their attendance.

Tickets are 7$ and include admission and one drink ticket. In addition, MAME will be providing over 100 cocktails throughout the event.

Spots are limited, if you are interested in attending please fill in this google form and pay with the Yapsody link.
