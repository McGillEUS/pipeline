Accelerated Machine Learning Bootcamp Applications

December 20
Academic
Application

Are you an undergrad with a strong foundation in mathematics and computer science?

Apply to join the first cohort of « MAIS 202: Accelerated Introduction to Machine Learning » in Winter 2019! Through real-time feedback, we’ll sharpen up your ML fundamentals and put you on track to complete your first ever ML project.

U0/U1s are strongly encouraged to apply! Check out the McGill AI Society Facebook event page for the class syllabus and more information!

 

January Orientation 2019 - Volunteer with CL&E

January 7
Involvement
Event

Winter is coming and so are the new January admits! Campus Life & Engagement (CL&E) is currently recruiting student volunteers to help us on Discover McGill, on Monday, January 7, 2019, 4:30 - 7:30 PM, in the Leacock Building.

As part of the event, a student panel titled "What I Wish I'd Known Starting University/Grad School" will be hosted for both undergraduates and graduate students, respectively. If you would like to share your experience at McGill as a first-year, please fill out the Panelist Application Form. If you would rather help out as a logistical volunteer, please fill out the Orientation Volunteer Form. Food will be provided!

Please respond as soon as possible, but hopefully by Monday, December 10. If you have any questions, please contact us at cle@mcgill.ca.

Reopening of Chatline!

December 4
Service
Notice

Whether you’re stressed about exams, walking home alone, or need a pizza recommendation, McGill Students’ Nightline is here for you. We are a confidential, anonymous, and non-judgmental listening service run by McGill students. We offer various forms of support including; active listening, information, referrals and crisis management.

After a few months of technical difficulties, we are excited that our Chatline will be up and running again on December 4th. You can contact us via chat here or by phone at 514-398-6246, every night between 6pm and 3am. You can talk to us about anything, so no matter what’s on your mind, pick up the phone or message us - we’re here for you. Our services will be available until the last day of exams (December 20, 2018) and will start up at the beginning of next semester (January 8, 2019).

 

McGill Job Shadowing Application

December 16
Occupational
Application

Not exactly sure what industry you want to work in? Nervous about learning the hard way after a full 4 month internship? Well, the McGill Job Shadowing Program is a great way to get a taste for what your dream job could look like before you actually commit! In this program you will be paired up with a McGill Alumni in your industry of interest, and spend a few days over Reading Week following them around at their job. Applications are due December 16! January Orientation 2019 - Looking for panelists

January 7
Involvement
Event

Campus Life & Engagement (CL&E) is in the midst of planning Discover McGill for the January Admit Students, taking place on Monday, January 7, 2019. We are currently recruiting students who are interested in sharing their experience at McGill with the new incoming class, during a special panel called “What I Wish I’d Known Starting University”. We believe that the new students will benefit greatly from listening to their seniors and will be encouraged to be more involved. The panel will be held from 6:30 – 7:30 PM, in the Leacock Building. If you would like to share your experience at McGill as a first-year, please fill out the Panelist Application Form, by mid-December. If you have any questions, please contact us at cle@mcgill.ca.
