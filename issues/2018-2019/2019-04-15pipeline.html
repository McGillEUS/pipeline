Peer Support Circle at Mac Campus

April 12
Wellness
Event

The Peer Support Centre presents the Peer Support Circle, a 3-week long drop-in support group. The Peer Support Centre offers confidential, non-judgemental, active listening support to all students, and has extended its service to offer a support group to students at Macdonald campus. Each session is drop-in and open to all students, undergraduate and graduate. Students are welcome to attend any or all of the sessions, no sign-up necessary.

The focus of the group will be de-stress, where participants will have the opportunity to share about an ongoing stressors, which could include academic stress, interpersonal problems, mental health, loneliness, etc. In addition to being able to share concerns and receive support, participants will have the opportunity to receive tools and resources from fellow participants and facilitators.

The group will be facilitated by a member of the Peer Support Centre, who is also a student and understands the concerns faced by the participants, and will aim to provide a confidential and welcoming environment.

Dates & Location:
Friday March 29th 11:30am-1:00pm - Eco Niche (Centennial Centre room 118)
Friday April 5th 11:30am-1:00pm - Faculty Lounge (MS2-022)
Friday April 12th 11:30am-1:00pm - Eco Niche (Centennial Centre room 118)

EUS Webmaster

Job Board
April 11
Involvement
Application

EUS Webmasters work on web services that the EUS hosts. They create and maintain various EUS websites while also contributing to the re-design of existing ones. Examples of websites webmasters may work on: the room booking service, the Frosh website, websites for other EUS events. Apply now if you have an interest in getting applied knowledge in any of the following technologies: Github, Amazon Web Services, React.js, Angular, Flask, Python, Javascript, MySQL databases, GraphQL, PHP, HTML, CSS.
