- Have experience with Finite Element Models and/or Thermal Analysis

Apply to Sit on the Sustainable Projects Fund Governance Council

February 22
Involvement
Application

The Sustainability Projects Fund (SPF) is looking for a SSMU representative to sit on its Governance Council (GC) starting in Summer/Fall 2019 for a two-year mandate. The SSMU representative would be one of eight GC members, who ensure that the SPF meets its mandate to build a culture of sustainability on McGill campuses. The GC evaluates applications for sustainability projects on campus using the SPF Evaluation Criteria.

Each representative is selected based on their level of engagement within McGill University; their understanding of sustainability as a whole and within the McGill community; and the complementarity of their expertise in relation to the other GC members. Members commit to attending a 2-hour meeting each month during the academic year and to reading all meeting documents in advance (~3 hours of preparation).

Please send an e-mail to krista.houser@mcgill.ca , with the subject line “SPF Governance Council Vacancy” and a brief paragraph expressing their interest, and relevant experience by February 22, 2019.
