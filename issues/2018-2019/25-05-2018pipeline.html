Get Involved with CFES Congress 2019
Application
Involvement

Congress is the CFES’ (Canadian Federation of Engineering Students) week-long annual general meeting, which hosts about 200 Engineering students from all over Canada. McGill will be hosting Congress in Montreal from January 2-7, 2019, which means McGill Engineering students will organize and execute all aspects of the conference and will have the chance to showcase the city of Montreal.

Want to be an integral part of the team that will organize Congress 2019? We are currently recruiting members to fulfil our executive and coordinator positions:

- VP Finance
- VP Sponsorship
- Sponsorship Coordinator
- Operations Coordinator

Please contact us if you would like to know more about Congress, the positions, or the type of commitment involved. Fill out the application form here!

Engineering Adventure Committee (EAC) Chair
Application
Involvement
May 27

EAC is a committee dedicated to organizing diverse events that showcase the variety of fun things Montreal has to offer, with a specific focus on non alcohol-centric events. Past events have included a Food Crawl, games night at Randolph's, trips to La Ronde, camping, rafting, visits to Escape Rooms, rock climbing, snow tubing, laser tag and more!!

The EUS is looking for the next EAC chair to lead the committee in planning and executing more fun, creative events for the 2018-2018 school year!

The application for chair is due on Sunday, May 27th, 2018 at 5:00PM. If you want to talk more about the position or have any questions, please contact vpinternal@mcgilleus.ca.

Application form available here.
