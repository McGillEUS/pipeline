The 2016 EUS Handbook is in production! The handbook is a guide to engineering life at McGill, covering tips & tricks, how to get involved, and an agenda to plan out your year.

Looking for a Content Curator

Do you think you know the EUS? Are you interested in shaping the interaction between EUS and students? Are you in love with the written word as an art form? Do you like 4 question lead-ins?

Apply now to be the Content Curator for the EUS! This is a developing position, so YOU can help determine the specific roles. Potential responsibilities includes editing descriptions of EUS groups, updating news on the McGill EUS website, and curating content for the EUSwiki.
If you're interested, please contact Malcolm McClintock, VP Communications.
