The McGill Engineering Techfair: Fall 2016 Edition

McGill’s largest on-campus career fair is back!
When: Wednesday, September 28th & Thursday September 29, 10:00am – 3:00pm
Dress: Professional attire
Where: New Residence Hall (3625 Avenue Du Parc)
Meet over 100 engineering firms looking to hire young, talented McGill engineers for full-time jobs and internships. Not looking for employment or internships? Come practice your interpersonal and networking skills, meet company representatives and get a better idea of your field of employment and get a competitive edge over other applicants. Only McGill students and alumni will be permitted entry. There will be different companies present on each day. For more usefull information such as companies participating, different worshops to increase your chances of success and much more, check out our Facebook page  or our website .

Involvement and Recruitment Director Applications

The EUS is constantly expanding and, in order to run the various, clubs, committees, departments, design teams, councils and more, we need your help. The involvement and recruitment director will directly oversee the recruitment process in order to assure that all positions are fairly advertised and fairly chosen. Furthermore, it is the role of the director promote transparency within our community by helping to host EUS open forums.
This is a new role and it will be up to you as well as the EUS President and VP Clubs & Admin to fully define it.

Please submit the application form by Monday, October 3rd at 11:59PM.

Do the Games !!

What is the engineering games ? It's an engineering competition combining academic, social, and athletic skills between Quebec Universities. It's the best way to meet fellow engineers from outside McGill, test your competence in your discipline, and have fun all at the same time.
When is it happening ? January 3rd to 7th in Montreal. We are looking for more people to join the fun ! #DoTheGames #FaitesLesJeux

Email us at enggames@mcgilleus.ca for more information.
