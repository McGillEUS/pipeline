Fun Fact:

Sir Martin Frobisher, who's landing in Newfoundland in 1578 marks the beginning of the celebration of Thanksgiving, had his heart buried in St.Andrew's Church of Plymouth and his body in St.Giles of London. This is a practice known as heart-burial, which has also been practiced on notable figures such as Frédéric Chopin, Thomas Hardy, and
Anne Boleyn.

Robogals is recruiting

Robogals is an international, student-run organization that aims to increase the number of girls and women studying engineering, technology, and science. We do this by engaging girls in these topics from a young age with school visits and other fun activities using LEGO NXT robots. Robogals North America is looking to expand to more universities this year, including McGill!

Six undergraduate or graduate women will form the executive committee for the chapter. They will join other women from participating universities and attend the Robogals SINE (Seminars Inducting New Executives) in January 2017.

If you would like to help start the Robogals chapter at McGill, please apply for the position of your choice here before the 21th of November:https://docs.google.com/a/robogals.org/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeVaQNJTGQlYOp6pLl3hagYEXAMQWHzGR8FsxhITD_GcyqZkw/viewform

Peace by Peace Volunteer Opportunity

Peace x PEACE is looking for classroom volunteers!

Peace by PEACE Montreal is a student-run volunteer organization based out of McGill University. Our volunteers work in elementary school classrooms around Montreal and facilitate workshops on issues such as conflict resolution, inter-cultural harmony, and inner power. Through games, role-play, activities and discussions, students are introduced to cooperative problem-solving skills in a fun and interactive environment. Our program consists of nine one-hour long workshops, each focusing on a different theme. At the end of the in-class component, all of our students celebrate their accomplishments together at our annual Peace Festival.

Volunteering is one hour a week during the winter semester!

Interested? You can attend our open information sessions in the Education Lounge (in the basement) on:
Wednesday November 9th from 4-6PM
If you cannot attend the information session and are interested in volunteering, please email us as soon as possible, as training sessions will be starting soon.
Or send us an email peacebypeacemontreal@gmail.com

You can find further details on our website: http://peacebypeacemontreal.weebly.com
