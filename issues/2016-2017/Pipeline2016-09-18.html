Join the EmPOWEr Program

POWE’s EmPOWEr program is a mentorship program between new and more experienced POWE engineering students. EmPOWEr pairs POWE members in U2/U3/U4 with members in U0/U1 with the goal of creating a relationship in which the mentor can for instance share study techniques, give advice on how to cope with classes and stress, and much more.

If you are an upper year student ready to help guide a new student through the first year of McGill and have a great experience at the same time, sign up here

If you are a new student who is interested in the valuable advice from an experienced mentor and being introduced to McGill Engineering and POWE in the best way possible, sign up here.

McGill 3D Printing Design Team (M3DP) is looking for a new VP Internal!

McGill's newest design team, the McGill 3D Printing Design Team (M3DP) is looking for a new VP Internal! M3DP provides students with the opportunity to gain hands-on experience in 3D printing, through projects and competitions ranging from printing parts to designing actual printers.

As VP Internal, your main responsibilities would include acting as the main contact for the team’s faculty advisor, and coordinating internal events such as info sessions and recruitment.

If you are interested in becoming part of the M3DP exec team, apply here by Friday, September 16th, 11:59 PM. You can inbox our M3DP facebook page or email m3dp@mcgilleus.ca if you want to find out more about M3DP and the VP Internal position.
