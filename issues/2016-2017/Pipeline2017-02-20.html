Help Us Help You

Resource
Sustainability

Reboot McGill is looking to collect any personal donations consisting of computers, laptops and peripherals that are broken or no longer in use. We fix the equipment we receive and donate it back to members of the McGill student body. Don't know how to recycle your electronics? We also arrange for the proper disposal of all usable equipment and scrap. Please note that McGill owned equipment should be donated to Hazardous Waste Management. In need of a computer, laptop or peripheral device free of charge? Contact us at reboot@mcgilleus.ca and we'll see what we can do! Our office hours are weekdays 11am to 4pm.
