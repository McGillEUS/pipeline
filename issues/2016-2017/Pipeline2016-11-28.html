Sustainability Applied Research Grant!

December 2
Funding
Sustainability

Calling all McGill engineers! Did you know you can get funding to conduct your own research on sustainability at McGill? It’s called Sustainability Applied Student Research—an opportunity for students to address the needs of the McGill community for academic credit in a hands-on way; bridging the gap between theories taught in the classroom and their practical application. For this round of funding, we’re looking for research projects that would explore waste management at McGill. Learn more about the ASR process and apply by December 2nd!
 

JAMAA Case Competition

November 30
Event
Occupational

Have you always wanted to change the world? Are you looking for a stimulating challenge? Then JAMAA's annual case competition is right for you! This year's competition consists of finding solutions to a problem plaguing Africa right now: The management of electric and electronic waste in Ghana.  Teams of 2 to 4 people work togeher to propose a plausible solution. The winning teams stands a chance of getting financial support for their project, as well as valuable support and assistance in realizing the project.

Send your name(s) to jamaa.polymtl@gmail.com to register, JAMAA is accepting team registrations until November 30th. Good luck!
