President Application Open: Reboot

The EUS is currently searching for someone to take charge of rebooting Reboot. Reboot took what was useless-to-someone computer hardware and swapped, fixed and patched pieces together into new systems: a sustainable computer recycling team. There are still old computers popping up which people don’t want thrown in the trash, there are still people who could use free computers, and there are still plenty of people who would love to learn about computers and help others.

What's Needed
*Someone who wants to understand Reboot and its mission for sustainability.
*Someone with enough time and determination to make the necessary investments.
*Someone with a vision for Reboot, willing to take on challenges, and lead a team

While the club unfortunately didn’t hand on the torch once the original members left, everything to keep this club alive is still there. You will have near absolute autonomy in how you run things. If interested, please contact the <a href="mailto:it.director@mcgilleus.ca">EUS IT Director</a>.

EWB: Food Systems VL Application

Are you passionate about sustainable food systems in Montreal? Engineers Without Borders McGill is looking for a Food Systems Venture Lead. Engineers Without Borders is a Canadian NGO that creates systemic change and raises awareness in the community through university chapters across Canada. The Food Systems Venture associated with EWB McGill undertakes projects to improve the food consumption and education needs of McGill and Montreal. We are looking for someone who understands the many aspects of food production, distribution, and consumption. This is an opportunity to develop your interests in food systems and work to affect real change in the Montreal Community under an established organization. Read the attached application form for more information. The deadline to submit is October 28th. https://goo.gl/forms/cs0ptBUtG8eFNyJT2
