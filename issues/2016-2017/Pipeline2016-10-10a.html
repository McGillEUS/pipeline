Student Space Fund (SSF) Applications - Call for Submissions

Applications to the Fall 2016 Student Space Fund (SSF) are open and will be accepted until Sunday, October 16th at 5:00 PM. The purpose of the SSF is to maintain and improve engineering undergraduate space through capital expenditures. The fund can be used for anything from buying furniture for a departmental lounge to buying plants for the hallways! Any undergraduate engineering student, department, or group can apply to the fund. The applications can be found at https://airtable.com/shrMu288TTZjCcNpW. For questions regarding submissions please contact the EUS Funds Director at funds.director@mcgilleus.ca

ECC&POWE: Building your Network with LinkedIn

In this workshop, you will learn:
- What LinkedIn is and why is it an good social media tool for students looking for employment
- How to create a great LinkedIn Profile that will get the attention of potential employers
- How to benefit the most from a professional network adding Groups, Companies, etc (using discussions, recommendations, skills, etc.)
- How to search Alumni, the advantages of adding your peers
