Mental Health Committee is Recruiting!

Mental Health Committee is a new committee dedicated to improving wellness in the engineering student body. We will be hosting events and workshops throughout the school year to increase awareness and connect students to resources. We will also be working towards long-term changes within engineering to improve student mental health. If you are interested in joining, please email mental.health@mcgilleus.ca
 

2016 OAP Kiosk Registration is now Open

Please fill out this form if you want your EUS Committee, Club, or Design Team to have a Kiosk in Three Bares Park during operating hours of OAP.  Whether its to recruit more members or to promote an event, an OAP Kiosk is a great chance to get your name out there!  Applications are due Sunday, August 14 at 11:59 pm EST.
For any questions, contact us at oap@mcgilleus.ca
