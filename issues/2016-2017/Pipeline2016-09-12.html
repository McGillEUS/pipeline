McGill Rocket Team is Recruiting!

The Rocket Team is a team that builds rockets that are tasked with delivering a 10 lb payload to 10000ft above ground level. The team develops the entire thing from scratch, including its own flight computer, fuselage, engine, simulators, and an autonomous ejection system, designed to deploy parachutes for recovery. The sub-teams you could join are: Aerodynamics, Avionics, Structure, Payload, Propulsion, Business, and Multimedia. The competition is in Utah every year, with over 70 teams attending from all over the world.

McGill Rocket Team is always looking for new members! If you are interested, and would like to know more about the rockets and the competition, please come to our information session on Wednesday, September 14th, at 6pm in FDA 5.
