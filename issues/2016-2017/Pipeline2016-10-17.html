Poetry for Thought:

As consequent from store of summer rains,
Or wayward rivulets in autumn flowing,
Or many a herb-lined brook's reticulations,
Or subterranean sea-rills making for the sea,
Songs of continued years I sing.

Life's evermodern rapids first, (soon to blend,
With the old streams of death.)

Some threading Ohio's farm-fields or the woods,
Some down Colorado's canons from sources of perpetual snow,
Some half-hid in Oregon, or away southward in Texas,
Some in the north finding their way to Erie, Niagara, Ottawa,
Some to Atlantica's bays, and so to the great salt brine.

In you whoe'er you are my book perusing,
In I myself, in all the world, these currents flowing,
All, all toward the mystic ocean tending.

Application Open: Reboot President

November 4
Application
Involvement

The EUS is currently searching for someone to take charge of rebooting Reboot. Reboot took what was useless-to-someone computer hardware and swapped, fixed and patched pieces together into new systems. They made sure that nothing went to waste, that a need was filled without needless expense and they learned a lot doing it. There’s still old computers popping up which people don’t want thrown in the trash, there’s people who could use free computers, and there’s plenty of people who would love to learn about computers and help others.

What's Needed
*Someone who wants to understand Reboot and its mission.
*Someone with enough time and determination to make the necessary investments.

Your Job
*One time - Establish a presence and spread the word: Reboot is back in action.
*One time - Find, update and create financial/governance information, passwords and inventory.
*Continuous - Manage the office space allocated to Reboot.
*Continuous – Recruit, lead, and encourage team members.

While the club unfortunately didn’t hand on the torch once the original members left, everything to keep this club alive is still there. You will have near absolute autonomy in how you run things. If interested, please contact the EUS IT Director at it.director@mcgilleus.ca

Project Management - MIAE Developmental Course

October 20
Event
Occupational

This Thursday from 6 to 7:30 pm, in MD 267, join MIAE for a developmental course on Project Management in aerospace industry. Sylvian Beaudry, a Senior Project Manager from CAE, will present students methodologies and strategies implemented in larger aerospace firms.

Fresh portuguese chicken sandwich/wraps, custard tarts and soft drink will be provided.

He completed a bachelor in Mechanical Engineering from École Polytechnique de Montréal and a master’s in Project Management from UQAM.

EWB: Food Systems VL Application

October 28
Application
Involvement

Are you passionate about sustainable food systems in Montreal? Engineers Without Borders McGill is looking for a Food Systems Venture Lead. Engineers Without Borders is a Canadian NGO that creates systemic change and raises awareness in the community through university chapters across Canada. The Food Systems Venture associated with EWB McGill undertakes projects to improve the food consumption and education needs of McGill and Montreal. We are looking for someone who understands the many aspects of food production, distribution, and consumption. This is an opportunity to develop your interests in food systems and work to affect real change in the Montreal Community under an established organization. Read the attached application form for more information. The deadline to submit is October 28th.
