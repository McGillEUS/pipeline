Student Space Fund (SSF) Applications - Call for Submissions
October 16
Application
Space

Applications to the Fall 2016 Student Space Fund (SSF) are open and will be accepted until Sunday, October 16th at 5:00 PM. The purpose of the SSF is to maintain and improve engineering undergraduate space through capital expenditures. The fund can be used for anything from buying furniture for a departmental lounge to buying plants for the hallways! Any undergraduate engineering student, department, or group can apply to the fund. The applications can be found here. For questions regarding submissions please contact the EUS Funds Director at funds.director@mcgilleus.ca
