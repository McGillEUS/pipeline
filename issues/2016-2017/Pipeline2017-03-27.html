Final Exam Prep Sessions

Academic
Sponsor

Feeling unprepared for your finals?

Don't worry - Prep101 has got your back! Our educational partner will be holding sessions designed to get you prepped for your final exams! We've been hearing great things from those of you who used them for your midterms and for last semester's finals. Final exam prep sessions are offered in the following courses: CHEM 120, MATH 141, MATH 262, MATH 263, MATH 264, and PHYS 142.

Prep101 prep sessions are focused on going through lots of past final exam questions with detailed explanations. We highly recommend you take advantage of these sessions.

Apply to be TechWeek Chair 2018

March 28
Application
Involvement

Do you want to run the next TechWeek? You can apply now for the position of TechWeek Chair for 2018! TechWeek is a week-long of events hosted in January by the EUS, aimed to explore where an engineering degree can take you. From TED-like professor TechTalks, to debates, workshops, and cutting-edge technology demos, TechWeek is the place to showcase all opportunities available in engineering at McGill and in Montreal.

Any returning student is encouraged to apply! For more information about the position, please get in touch with this year's TechWeek Chair, Afreen Aliya, at techweek@mcgilleus.ca. 

To apply, please fill out this application form and email your CV and application form to the EUS VP Academic. Deadline to apply is this Tuesday, March 28th at 5pm.
