Hema-Quebec Blood Drive

Every 80 seconds, someone in Québec needs blood. It takes 1,000 blood donations every day to meet the needs of hospitalized patients.

The Faculty of Medicine at McGill, in collaboration with McGill Engineering Undergraduate Society (EUS), invites you to a blood drive on September 12th to September 14th, from 9:30 a.m. to 5:30 p.m., at the EUS Common Room in McConnell Engineering Building, located at 3480 Rue University, Montréal.

Who can give?

Donors must be at least 18 years of age with no upper age cut-off. You can check your eligibility by calling Donor Services at 1 800 847-2525 or by going online at www.hema-quebec.qc.ca For a successful blood drive experience, eat a good meal and drink plenty of fluids beforehand.

Give blood. Give life.

Equity Co-Commissioner Application Open

The Equity Co-Commissioner shall be in charge of chairing the EUS Equity Committee and promote diversity, inclusion and equity in engineering at McGill. The Co-Commissioner shall also facilitate equity workshops with relevant groups within the EUS. Finally, the Co-Commissioner shall also manage the EUS equity complaint process

If you are interested in the position, please send an email to president@mcgilleus.ca for the application form. Application ends on September 9th at 11:59pm

The Engineering Peer Tutoring Service Is Hiring!

The application period will begin on August 15 and end on September 9. Interviews will be conducted on the second week of class September 12th-16th. To apply, applicants must specify which classes they want to tutor as well as send a copy of their transcript, and resume to epts@mcgilleus.ca by the deadline. Applicants will be contacted about interviews by the latest on September 11th.

In order to be eligible all applicants must meet the following requirements:
• Currently be an undergraduate student in the faculty of Engineering
• Have completed their first year at McGill
• Have achieved at least an A- in the classes they tutor
