EUS Elections 2017

January 27
Application
Involvement
Important

It’s never too late to apply to be part of the EUS 2017-2018 Exec Team; the Nomination Period has now been extended to Tuesday January 31st by 5pm! It is always a wonderful opportunity to be part of the EUS and strive to make McGill an amazing place for the future engineers! All forms and details can be found at: elections.mcgilleus.ca.

Nominations are open to all positions with an exception of the position of the VP External and VP Finance. Kindly submit VP Finance application electronically to president@mcgilleus.ca by Friday, February 10th. Candidates are strongly encouraged to discuss the responsibilities of these positions with a current executive.

 

Reboot: Help Us Help You

Resource
Tech

Reboot McGill is looking to collect any computers, laptops and peripherals that are broken or no longer in use. We fix the equipment we receive and donate it back to members of the McGill community. Don’t know how to recycle your electronics? We also arrange for the proper disposal of all usable equipment and scrap. In need of a computer, laptop or peripheral device free of charge? Contact us at reboot@mcgilleus.ca and we’ll see what we can do! Our office hours are weekdays 11am to 4pm in Ferrier 218.

CEEC Conference

March 2-4
Event
Conference

The Commerce and Engineering Environmental Conference (CEEC) delegate applications are out and are due this Thursday, February 2nd at 11:59PM!

CEEC is a conference hosted at Queen's University from March 2-4. The theme this year is the Modern Ecosystem, which will focus on redefining the relationship between industry and the environment for the modern world. The weekend will be filld with workshops, speakers, and networking opportunities!

For more information as well as a link to the application, check out the McGill-specific facebook event here!  If you have any questions, e-mail seam.vpadmin@mcgilleus.ca
 

The CUBE 3D-Printing Service is Hiring

Application
Occupational

The EUS is hiring a part-time student technician at The Cube 3D printing service for Spring/Summer 2017! If you’re a second-or-third year student with an interest in additive manufacturing and 3D scanning, please submit your CV and a brief personal statement (telling us about your experience, why you'd like to get involved, etc.) to aml.mecheng@mcgill.ca with the subject line “Spring 2017 Student Tech Applicant." No experience is required, however it is recommended the applicant has great customer service skills and general mechanical/electrical aptitude. Experience with CAD software is a plus.

The Cube is ALSO looking for somebody with web development experience to update our website and migrate it to the EUS server over the summer 2017. If interested, please include relevant experience in your application.
