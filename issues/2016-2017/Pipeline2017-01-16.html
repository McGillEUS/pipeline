Fun Fact:

Sir Martin Frobisher, who's landing in Newfoundland in 1578 marks the beginning of the celebration of Thanksgiving, had his heart buried in St.Andrew's Church of Plymouth and his body in St.Giles of London. This is a practice known as heart-burial, which has also been practiced on notable figures such as Frédéric Chopin, Thomas Hardy, and

EUS Executive Nomination Period

As some of you might already know, the 2016-2017 EUS Elections is fast approaching; the Nomination Period is now open until Friday January 27th by 5pm! It is always a wonderful opportunity to be part of the EUS and strive to make McGill an amazing place for the future engineers.

Nominations are open to all positions with the exception of the VP Finance, whose deadline will be Friday, February 10th, and is selected by a selection committee. Kindly submit VP Finance application electronically to <a href="mailto:president@mcgilleus.ca">president@mcgilleus.ca</a>.

All forms and details can be <a href="http://elections.mcgilleus.ca/">found here</a>. In case of any concerns, please contact <a href="mailto:cro@mcgilleus.ca">cro@mcgilleus.ca</a>.
