The Engineering Peer Tutoring Service Is Hiring!

The application period will begin on August 15 and end on September 9. Interviews will be conducted on the second week of class September 12th-16th. To apply, applicants must specify which classes they want to tutor as well as send a copy of their transcript, and resume to epts@mcgilleus.ca by the deadline. Applicants will be contacted about interviews by the latest on September 11th.

In order to be eligible all applicants must meet the following requirements:
• Currently be an undergraduate student in the faculty of Engineering
• Have completed their first year at McGill
• Have achieved at least an A- in the classes they tutor

 

EUS Chief Electoral Officer Application

Are you interested in student politics, elections, and other exciting items such as a referenda? Yes? Yes? Yes!

Apply through this form.

Examples of duties include responsibility for overseeing the U1 Representative's elections, the EUS elections in February, and aiding departmental elections in Winter 2016. Additionally, the CRO is tasked with overseeing the Fall and Winter Referendum periods!

Please note: Chief Returning Officer may not be a candidate in any election, nor propose referendum questions during their term.

POWE Presents: Get to know McKinsey!

Are you a female engineering student interested in pursuing a career in the consulting industry? Have you ever wondered what amazing job opportunities are offered at McKinsey?

McKinsey is organizing a networking event in collaboration with POWE and would love for you to join! You will then have the chance to ask questions, and chat with them personally!

There are only 35 spots, so be sure to sign up at this link as soon as possible.
