Conference on Diversity in Engineering Delegate Application

Interested in learning about diversity in engineering with eng students from across Canada? McGill is hosting this year's CFES Conference on Diversity in Engineering & the EUS is sending a delegation (10 students)!

The conference will be held November 18th-20th 2016 @ the Marriott Chateau Champlain, in the heart of Montreal. The CDE will bring together 200 delegates from across Canada and numerous industry leaders to discuss issues minorities face in engineering, the importance of diversity, and what you can do to make the engineering profession more inclusive and accessible to everyone.

Applications to attend the conference will be accepted from October 3rd-7th. The application form can be found here: http://tiny.cc/CDE-Application, and must be submitted to vpexternal@mcgilleus.ca.

Feel free to contact vpexternal@mcgilleus.ca with any questions or stop by the EUS office on Monday or Wednesday from 11:30-1:00pm.

Make sure to check out the CDE website for more information: http://2016.cde.cfes.ca/

Best of luck!

Sustainability in Aerospace - MIAE Speaker Series

Aviation and sustainability should go hand in hand to reduce the impact of air travel and aerospace manufacturing on climate change. Over the last 50 years there has been a 70% reduction of fuel-burn/passenger-Km, and the aviation industry continues to have a commitment to achieve further emissions reduction.

MIAE will be hosting Jim Banks as a guest speaker to highlight the importance of sustainability in aerospace product development. Jim is an accomplished sustainability expert with proficiency in new product development, strategic foresight and innovation with a track record of developing and implementing initiatives that build cultures of sustainability within major aerospace manufacturers(Bombardier, Pratt and Whitney Canada).

The event will be held on Thursday October 6th at 6:00 pm in room MD267 in the Macdonald Engineering building.

All students regardless of membership are welcome to attend. Refreshment will be provided.

Event link: https://www.facebook.com/events/186620438413277/
