EUS Chief Electoral Officer Application

Are you interested in student politics, elections, and other exciting items such as a referenda? Yes? Yes? Yes!

Apply through this form.

Examples of duties include responsibility for overseeing the U1 Representative's elections, the EUS elections in February, and aiding departmental elections in Winter 2016. Additionally, the CRO is tasked with overseeing the Fall and Winter Referendum periods!

Please note: Chief Returning Officer may not be a candidate in any election, nor propose referendum questions during their term.

McGill Community Engagement Day

McGill’s Social Equity and Diversity Education Office (SEDE) will be hosting and celebrating the 5 th anniversary of Community Engagement Day (CED) on and around Thursday September 29 th , 2016! CED presents students, faculty, and staff with an opportunity to engage with the broader Montreal community by offering over 30 projects and activities in partnership with community organizations. CED is about solidarity, not charity – by promoting multi-directional engagement, we wish to serve as an ally to community organizations.
Registration begins here on Monday, August 15th.
For more information please contact Arabella Colombier, Program Coordinator.

 

Mental Health Committee is Recruiting!

Mental Health Committee is a new committee dedicated to improving wellness in the engineering student body. We will be hosting events and workshops throughout the school year to increase awareness and connect students to resources. We will also be working towards long-term changes within engineering to improve student mental health. If you are interested in joining, please email mental.health@mcgilleus.ca
