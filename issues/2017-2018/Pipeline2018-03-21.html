EUS Mental Health Commissioner
The EUS Mental Health Commissioner's mission is to promote a mental health and wellness in the engineering community. This role includes:
- Organize committee meetings.
- Promote positive mental health and wellness practices in the engineering community.
- Oversee committee projects and initiatives.

Application
Due Date: Monday March 26th, 2018 at 5pm


EUS Clubs Representative
The EUS Clubs Rep's main responsibilities include:
- Maintain a line of communication between Clubs and the EUS, and participate in Clubs Council, Clubs Fund Committee, the clubs audits and Student Space Fund Committee.
- Manage Clubs Hub.
- Facilitate the collection of clubs exit reports.

This role is a unique opportunity to work with EUS clubs and help the EUS support their growth.

Application
Due Date: Monday March 28th, 2018 at 5pm
 

IT Committee Applications are Open!
March 27
Application
Involvement

The application period is now open for positions on the EUS IT Committee for the upcoming academic year! The positions available are those of Webmaster, Wiki Curator, and TeN Manager. Each position is a fun way to develop your technical experience through real-world projects.

Webmaster - develop and maintain the EUS's network of 40+ websites; manage the server-side setup of *.mcgilleus.ca
TeN Manager - curate content for the EUS's digital signage network; maintain the hardware of the network (server set-up, computers and monitors)
Wiki Curator - write articles and encourage others to contribute articles to the growing repository of EUS knowledge, the EUS Wiki
A position on the IT Committee is one of the most rewarding involvement opportunities in the EUS. For more information, see the IT Committee Wiki Article or email IT Director. The brief application form is available here, and is due by Tuesday, March 27th (all other positions).
