Cooperathon 2017
Academic

The Cooperathon is an open innovation challenge. This “marathon of cooperation” aims at building innovative solutions to actual issues faced by our society. Cocreated by committed citizens, experts and entrepreneurs, these solutions target sustainable social impact. Are you a change-maker driven by an inspiring idea? Do you want to co-create your solution with large organizations, experts, citizens and other entrepreneurs? The Cooperathon gives you a unique opportunity to build a sustainable impact startup!


The Cooperathon is about three weeks long and you have the opportunity to either join an existing team, or make your own. There is over 75 000$ in prize money and constant exposure to big companies. You can find more information on the official website

Complimentary BEST Courses all over Europe - Applications
October 15
Application
Involvement

The Board of European Students of Technology (BEST) is inviting Canadian engineering student volunteers to apply to attend their complementary education courses all over Europe!

There are 4 courses offered this year, on the following topics: 

1) Aerospace technology (Bordeaux, France) 

2) Sustainability and entrepreneurship (Paris, France) 

3) Locomotive design (Ekaterinburg, Russia) 

4) Brewing & Distilling (Graz, Austria)

Each course is usually about 2 weeks in length. Apply to participate before Oct. 15.

Contact the EUS VP External for more information.

Hult Prize Challenge: Info & Networking Session Oct 12
October 12
Competition

CALLING ALL SOCIAL ENTREPRENEURS, ENGINEERS, DEVELOPMENT STUDENTS, ENVIRO SCIENCE LOVERS, OR ANYONE WITH A PASSION FOR POSITIVE CHANGE.

The Hult Prize competition is the incredible opportunity to win 1 Million dollars to fund a start up idea to solve a social problem! A team of students from McGill won the million dollars in 2013, and every year we send 1 team to compete in the regional finals.

This year’s challenge: Harnessing the power of energy to transform the lives of 10 million people!

If you think you have a potential idea, or if you just want to meet some passionate people & learn more about the competition, come to our info & networking session on October 12th @ 6pm in Bronfman's 3rd floor MBA lounge. Check out the Facebook event!
