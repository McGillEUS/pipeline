EUS Incident Response Form
Health
Important

The EUS now has an incident response form (with the option to stay anonymous) that you can fill out to report an incident to the EUS equity commissioners. Your submission can include anything equity-related, whether it happened at an EUS event, Frosh, off campus, in class, in a lab- etc. There’s no such thing as an invalid submission! Your submission will remain highly confidential and should you wish to be contacted, we will follow up with you within 24 hours of your submission. Click Here to check it out!

Vision 2020 Climate & Sustainability Action Plan Launch
Dec 1
Event

After months of consultations with more than 500 community members, McGill’s Climate & Sustainability Action Plan is set to be released this December! In addition to addressing the University's most pressing sustainability challenges, this is the first Plan specifically aimed at reducing McGill's carbon footprint. Join us in celebrating the launch of the Climate & Sustainability Action Plan on Friday, December 1st from 4:00-6:00pm in the Faculty Club with the students, faculty, and staff who are helping us blaze new trails.
