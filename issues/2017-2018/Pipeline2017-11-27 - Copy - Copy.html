WHAT IS CFES?
CFES stands for ‚ÄúCanadian Federation of Engineering Students‚Äù, an organization that represents and promotes Canadian undergraduate engineering students at the national and international level. CFES provides a platform for knowledge exchange, communication, and growth between engineering societies across Canada and works to improve the Canadian engineering student experience.

WHAT IS CONGRESS?
Congress is the CFES‚Äô week-long Annual General Meeting that hosts approx. 200 engineering students from 51 member schools. Congress 2019 will be hosted by McGill in Montreal from January 1-6. That means that McGill engineering students will organize and execute all aspects of the conference and will have the chance to showcase the city of Montreal.

WHAT DO YOU HAVE TO DO WITH IT?
Want to be an integral part of the team that will organize Congress 2019?? We are currently recruiting members to fulfill our executive positions:

Help Build McGill's First Satellite!
Application

The CSDC is a competition for Canadian Universities to build nano-satellites under the CubeSat specification. The MSG CSDC design team is currently building a 3U CubeSat with a StarTracker payload. This is a great, hands-on experience to help build McGill's FIRST satellite while learning about spacecraft design and standards, challenges in aerospace engineering and how to engineer un-manned systems.

You should apply if you:
- Are interested in aerospace development, specifically satellite design.
- Want to get hands on experience building electric circuits and integrating them.
- Want to learn how to write reliable, low-level software that spans multiple disciplines.
- Are self-motivated and able to work both in an independent manner and as part of a team.

All applicants welcome; previous experience with design teams or projects is an asset.
