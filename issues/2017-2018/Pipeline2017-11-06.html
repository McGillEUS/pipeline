NOBExSEAM Present: The Business of Renewable Energy
Nov 7
Event
Career

The future of energy is sustainable. Where do we stand now? How will business, engineering, and sustainability come together to create a cleaner world?

Join NOBE and SEAM for a panel discussion on the renewable energy industry. This evening will feature four professionals from Enercon, Insejys, GE, and Mackinnon, Bennett & Co. discussing their careers in the renewable sector and what the opportunities are there for students looking to make a difference. MEC 2017 Edition
Nov 24-27
Event

The McGill Engineering Competition is back with its 2017 edition. The competition will take place on campus from November 24th to 27th. The MEC occurs over one weekend and includes many different competitions with the competition categories being: Junior Design, Senior Design, Consulting, Impromptu Debates, Communication, Re-engineering, Innovative Design and Graduate Scientific Research.

Winners get an all expenses paid trip to the Quebec Engineering Competition for a chance to compete on the provincial level. For more information about the event and to see killer memes see the event page. We will also be tabling in McConnel Hallway for the next two weeks.
 

NOBExECC - What is Technical Sales?
Nov 14
Event
Career

How do engineering firms find clients and ensure the product they are selling meets the demands of an evolving marketplace?

In the role of technical sales, engineers bridge the gap between their company and its clients, combining interpersonal skills and engineering understanding to bring projects into reality.

Join NOBE in learning what technical sales are all about from actual engineering graduates working in this role. We will be hosting a panel discussion followed by some light networking on November 14th from 5:30-7:00pm in the McConnell Engineering Basement (Common Room). Food and refreshments will be provided. Register on MyFuture > Events > Workshops (keywords: NOBE, ECC)

Electronics Donation Day
Nov 7
Event
SUSTAINABILITY

Got too many old electronics lying around? Not sure what to do with them or how to safely dispose of them? Reboot McGill is here to solve your problem! On Tuesday, November 7, 2017 from 12:00pm to 3:00pm, Reboot will be tabling near the Y-intersection/Lower Field to collect your old electronics.

Location: Near the Y-Intersection / Lower Field
Date: Tuesday, November 7, 2017
Time: 12:00 pm - 3:00 pm
This event is open to all: McGill Community & General Public

Any items considered reusable will be refurbished for the McGill Student Community, while the rest will be disposed of in a safe and responsible manner.

Accepted Electronics:
Laptops, Computers, Keyboards, Mice, Printers, etc.
*Reboot will only be receiving personal items. No items purchased by the University will be taken.

For more information or any inquiries email us.
