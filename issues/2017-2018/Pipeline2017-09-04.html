CPP is a 6 week core curriculum program designed to build the foundations of case cracking, gives you opportunity to do mock cases with consultants, and an opportunity to go on firm tours in Toronto this fall! PERSWADE
September 7
Application
Involvement

We are pleased to invite outstanding eligible students to apply for enrolment in the PERSWADE Program. PERSWADE aims at providing enhanced training opportunities to undergraduate students in the areas of wireless networks and systems (WNS) and their applications. More specifically, PERSWADE aims at improving their job readiness by instilling a well-gauged industrial orientation in their training experience. For more information on the PERSWADE Program, check out our website. Also, for more information on the application process, please refer to the Program Guide

 

All applications have to be submitted by email directly to yang.chen@emt.inrs.ca no later than September 7, 2017.
