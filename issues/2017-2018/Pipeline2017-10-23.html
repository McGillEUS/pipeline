Important

The McGill EngInE Programming Workshops
Oct 24/25
Event
Academic

Are you interested in creating your own websites and web applications?
Are you interested in working for or creating your own web startup?
The McGill Faculty of Engineering Innovation and Entrepreneurship (the McGill EngInE) hub is proud to announce our upcoming programming workshops!


With two experienced engineers who will lead these workshops, students will learn the foundational skills of front-end and back-end web application development, that is, a Full-Stack focus, by walking through the development of an AirBnB clone.


Any student enrolled at McGill’s Faculty of Engineering is eligible to participate. There are no formal prerequisites for these workshops but you must bring your laptop. If you took the workshops in January and March 2017, these workshops will cover much of the same material.

Registration is required.

Dates for these sessions:
Day 1: Tuesday, October 24, 2017, 5:30pm-8:00pm
Day 2: Wednesday, October 25, 2017, 5:30pm-8:00pm

Click here for more information about the workshops and to register.
