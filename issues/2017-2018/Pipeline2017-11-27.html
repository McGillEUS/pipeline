Final Exam Prep Sessions
Sponsor

Feeling unprepared for your finals?

Don't worry - Prep101 has got your back! Our educational partner will be holding sessions designed to get you prepped for your final exams! We've been hearing great things from those of you who used their service. Final exam prep sessions are offered in the following courses: CHEM 110, MATH 133, MATH 140, MATH 141, MATH 262, MATH 263, MATH 264, and PHYS 131.

Prep101 prep sessions are focused on going through lots of past final exam questions with detailed explanations. We highly recommend you take advantage of these sessions!

To sign up, go to our website.

EUS Survey on Alcohol Use at Events

Health
Important

Please fill out this survey on alcohol use at events among engineering students! Upon completion, you wll have the option of including your email for a chance to win one of several frostbite giftcards. 

The results of this survey will be used to gain a better understanding of how we can improve students' experiences at our events and what types of events engineering students want to see the EUS running. 
 

G-STORE is NOW SELLING VUA SANDWICHES
