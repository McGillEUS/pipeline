Suits U Application
Application
Involvement

Suits U is looking for motivated, engaging, and organized students to join our team!

We are a national non-profit organization dedicated to empowering students by providing them with affordable, high quality donated business apparel. We work with companies across Montreal to collect clothing that will be sold to students to raise money for charities in the city.

To learn more check out our website.

Involvement

Paid On-Campus 3D-printing Technician Opportunity
Sept 30
Application
Occupational

The Cube 3D-printing service is hiring a part-time student technician! If you're a second-or-third year student with an interest in additive manufacturing and 3D scanning, please submit your CV and a brief personal statement (telling us about your experience, why you'd like to get involved, etc.) via email with the subject line “Fall 2017 Student Tech Applicant" by September 30! 

No experience is required, however the successful applicant will have great customer service skills and general mechanical/electrical aptitude. Experience with CAD software is a plus.
