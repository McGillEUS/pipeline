Student Engsperience Survey
Survey
Important

The EUS is seeking to better understand the experiences and perceptions of students in our Faculty. To this end, we have created the Student Engsperience Survey. This survey, which has been beta-tested and refined to be completed in under 10 minutes, asks you to share your opinion regarding various aspects of typical engineering student life.

To express our gratitude for your participation in the survey, the first 550 individuals to fill out the survey will receive a free baby ice cream coupon redeemable at Frostbite. These coupons can be picked up in the EUS Office (MC 07) immediately after you have completed the survey. Additionally, you will be entered into a raffle to receive one of three $50 Amazon gift cards.

The deadline to fill out the survey is April 30th, but the baby ice cream coupons are only valid until April 12th. The survey can be found here.

We look forward to reviewing your responses!!
