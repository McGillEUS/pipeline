EUS Fall Referendum Campaigning and Voting Period
Oct 16-20
Important

ENGINEERS! The time has come for the Fall Referendum once again. Three referendum questions will be up for debate this period, regarding the following:

  1. The EUS general fee
  2. The Engineers Without Borders fee renewal
  3. The EUS Design Design Team fund fee

Why does this matter to you? Referendum questions make real changes to how much money engineering students give in fees, and where this money goes. It is absolutely CRITICAL that you take this chance to exercise your democratic voice, and VOTE October 24-27. 

Campaigning will occur October 16-20. If you have any referendum related questions, please email CRO, refer to the event page here, or visit the public Referendum Drive here.

Stay informed! Stay motivated! Stay in school!

Happy voting, EUS. 
 

Graduation Photos!
Oct 16-21, 25-27
Academic
Important

Attention all upcoming McGill graduates!!! Don't miss this great opportunity for Professional Graduation Portraits!

Professional graduation portraits will take place in the McConnell Engineering Building the week of October 16th and the 25th-27th. You must have your photo taken to be included in your respective graduating class mosaic. To signup:

1. Visit Lassman Studios.

2. Enter mcg18 in the school ID to access the schedules.

Find a time that is convenient for you and book it. New sessions get added weekly as the schedules fill up.

Included in your sitting are a minimum of 10 unique online poses from which you can view/order online and a digital copy of your graduating class mosaic. Both traditional grad portraits as well as some fun style shots (if desired) will be taken. Gowns, roses, mortorboard, graduating hoods, etc. will be provided on the day of your shoot. You can also choose from over 25 different backgrounds!

As an added incentive, a $10 credit towards a photo package will be added to your online profile and a 5x7 print and 20% off discount code will be added as an early order bonus.

Questions? contact Lassman

COMunity presents: OFF-CAMPUS Rez Warz Team
Application
Involvement

For the first time ever, we are adding a COMunity Off-Campus Team to Rez Warz! Rez Warz is a yearlong competition. Each month, teams from every Residence and COMunity compete in a chosen competition and earn points. The team with the most points is awarded in April! Rez Warz is about expressing team spirit, being active, having fun, and engaging with peers. Some Rez Warz events are: soccer, Ultimate Frisbee, improv, Iron Chef, dodgeball, pub night trivia, and basketball. Events like improv and Iron Chef have featured celebrity guest judges from the University faculty and the greater Montreal community.

Interested? Add yourself to the Facebook group or email us at OCCSS with the subject line: REZ WARZ Off-Campus Team! Please include your full name and McGill email in the email.

Feel free to invite your OFF-CAMPUS FIRST YEAR friends!
